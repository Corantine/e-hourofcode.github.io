About

Join us on January 14th, 2020 for our first ever Hour of Code event!

We will be ordering pizza for the attendees.

You will be able to learn basic programming and work alongside your friends!



The Hour of Code is a global movement by Computer Science Education Week and Code.org reaching tens of millions of students in 180+ countries through a one-hour introduction to computer science and computer programming.

We will be holding one here at EDHS on January 14th at Lunch!
